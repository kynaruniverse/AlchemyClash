ENT-001 // PRIMARY THREAT
The Black Table
Class: Organisation — Ancient

The Black Table is not a recent creation. They predate modern governments, modern borders, modern institutions. They have been present — quietly, invisibly — at every significant juncture in recorded history. Not causing events. Controlling the conditions that allow events to happen.

Their objective is control. Not of any single nation or resource, but of the layer itself — and of the people the layer produces. They catalogue those with abilities. They monitor bleed points. They suppress information and, when suppression fails, they suppress people.

Their internal structure is unknown to outsiders. A council is believed to exist at the highest level — a small number of individuals whose identities have never been confirmed. Below that, the structure is deliberately obscured, even to those within it. Most people who work for The Black Table do not know they do.

STATUS: ACTIVE
THREAT: EXTREME
ORIGIN: PREDATES RECORD
KNOWN TO PUBLIC: NO
ENT-002 // POPULATION
The Unawakened
Class: General — Latent

The majority of the world's population. People who carry the potential for abilities without knowing it — dormant, untriggered, unaware. The layer's increasing activity means more of them are waking up every day. Most have no framework for what is happening to them. Most have no one to tell.

The Black Table monitors trigger events closely. First awakening is the most dangerous moment — for the individual, and for everyone around them.

STATUS: INCREASING
THREAT: VARIABLE
AWARENESS: NONE
ENT-003 // EMERGING
The Unnamed
Class: Resistance — Forming

There is no organisation yet. No name, no structure, no leadership. What exists is a loose collection of individuals — some awakened, some simply suspicious — who have independently arrived at the same conclusion: something is being hidden, and whoever is hiding it does not have good intentions.

Whether they will organise into something coherent before The Black Table identifies and neutralises them is, at the time of this document's compilation, unknown.

STATUS: FORMING
THREAT TO BLACK TABLE: LOW — CURRENTLY
STRUCTURE: NONE YET

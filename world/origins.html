Origins of the Kynar Universe

Foundational Record — Classification Level: Visitor

The world most people inhabit is not the whole world. It never was. Beneath the surface of the present — beneath the cities, the institutions, the ordinary momentum of daily life — there is another layer. It has no official name. It has always been there. And for most of human history, almost no one knew.

The Layer

The hidden layer is not a place in the conventional sense. It does not have a postcode or coordinates. It is a dimension that runs parallel to our own — close enough that, at certain points, it bleeds through. A doorway that shouldn't open. A room that feels wrong in a way you cannot explain. A moment where something passes through the air and leaves a mark on the people nearby.

These bleed points are not random. They cluster around specific locations across the world — sites that share no obvious geographical or historical connection, except that they all sit at coordinates where the boundary between layers is thinner than it should be. Nobody knows why those locations were chosen. Nobody knows if they were chosen at all.

The Abilities

The layer's proximity to the surface world has consequences. People born near bleed points, or exposed to them at formative moments, sometimes develop abilities. Physical. Perceptual. Mental. Elemental. Dimensional. The range is wide and the origins vary — some abilities appear to be inherited, passed quietly through bloodlines for generations. Others seem to emerge from direct contact with the layer itself. A small number appear to arise from technological intervention.

Most people who carry these abilities have no idea they have them. The abilities lie dormant — waiting for a trigger, a pressure point, a moment of extremity that cracks something open. Across the world, right now, people are discovering what they are capable of for the first time. Most of them are not prepared. Most of them have no one to tell.

What Is Known and Who Knows It

The existence of the layer, and the abilities it produces, is not public knowledge. It is myth, rumour, dismissed. The people who know the truth — who have mapped the bleed points, catalogued the abilities, built entire systems of control around what they know — have spent a very long time ensuring it stays that way.

That organisation is referred to in this archive as The Black Table. Their full history is documented in DOC-KYN-003. What matters here is this: they did not discover the layer. They have simply always known about it. And they have never had any intention of sharing that knowledge.

The Present

Something has changed. The bleed points are becoming more active. More people are awakening to abilities they did not know they had. The layer is pushing closer to the surface — not catastrophically, not yet, but measurably. The instruments confirm it. The Black Table is aware. What they intend to do about it is not recorded in any document available at this clearance level.

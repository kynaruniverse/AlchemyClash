Known Bleed Points

Territory Map — Classification Level: Visitor — Partial Access

The following locations are sites where the boundary between the surface world and the hidden layer is measurably thinner than the surrounding area. They are spread across multiple real-world territories with no apparent geographical connection. What they share is documented below. Why they share it is not yet understood.

51.5074° N, 0.1278° W — LONDON, UK
The Underlift
Bleed Point — Urban / High Activity

Concentrated beneath the older boroughs of the city — the parts that were built before records were kept properly. The bleed here is not dramatic. It is persistent. A low hum. A door that opens onto a street that shouldn't be there. A section of tunnel on a line that doesn't appear on any map. Activity has increased significantly in the past eighteen months.

High Activity
40.7128° N, 74.0060° W — NEW YORK, USA
The Fracture
Bleed Point — Urban / Unstable

The bleed point here is unstable in a way that distinguishes it from others. It does not simply thin — it cracks. Brief, violent ruptures that expose the layer for seconds at a time before closing again. Three individuals with abilities have been documented emerging from within a two-block radius in the last year alone. The Black Table has a significant presence here.

Unstable
35.6762° N, 139.6503° E — TOKYO, JAPAN
The Still
Bleed Point — Urban / Dormant

Unlike other bleed points, the layer here does not push outward. It pulls. Those who spend extended time near the site report a sensation of being observed — not by anything present, but by something patient. Perceptual and dimensional abilities are disproportionately common among people who grew up in the surrounding area.

Monitoring
-33.8688° S, 151.2093° E — SYDNEY, AUSTRALIA
The Depth
Bleed Point — Coastal / Submerged

The only known bleed point that exists primarily below water. Discovered when a research team investigating unusual sonar readings disappeared for eleven hours and returned with no memory of the interval. All four individuals subsequently developed abilities. Current status: partially sealed by Black Table intervention. Effectiveness of sealing: disputed.

Partially Sealed
